The time has finally come for the stub CAOS.ORG site to expand into a real, grown-up web site. Well... maybe not entirely grown up: we still ahve lots to work out and add to the site, but at least we're putting more than just an under-construction notice on our domain. 

In the process we've learned a ton about Github Pages and Jekyll -- although again, there's plenty more to learn. Of course, it was important to us to use open-source tooling in an open-source environment, and to maintain a high standard for accessibility. As ever, we know we can always  do better, and hope you'll [let us know ]({{ "/contact/" | relative_url }})if you have any suggestions. 

For now, please join us in celebrating this next exciting step in the growth of CAOS!
